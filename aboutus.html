About Biopex

Who We Are

Biopex is more than just a supplier—we are a trusted partner for laboratories, clinics, hospitals, and healthcare providers. Established with a mission to advance health and scientific innovation, Biopex has steadily grown into a leading source for high-quality medical and laboratory products. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful principle: that access to world-class equipment and consumables should not be a barrier to great medicine or breakthrough research. As the biomedical landscape rapidly evolves, Biopex keeps pace—combining global sourcing power with personalized local service.

Over the years, we have developed deep relationships with renowned manufacturers and research organizations. These partnerships allow us to offer a catalog that spans basic laboratory consumables, advanced instrumentation, medical devices, diagnostics, and safety supplies. We serve a diverse customer base that includes universities, research institutes, reference labs, hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, and biotech startups. Our reach is global, but our support is personal. Whether you represent a large institution or a small clinic, you can count on tailored solutions, responsive service, and expert advice from the Biopex team.

At our core, we believe in reliability, integrity, and continuous improvement. Our commitment to quality is uncompromising. Every product we offer is vetted for safety, compliance, and performance, meeting the highest international standards. Our technical and sales teams receive ongoing training—ensuring up-to-date knowledge and an ability to help customers select the right solutions for their unique needs. We are passionate about making a positive impact on healthcare and science, responding dynamically to emerging challenges like infectious disease outbreaks or new laboratory techniques.

Our Mission

The mission of Biopex is to empower scientific discovery and quality patient care by providing reliable access to innovative medical and laboratory products. We envision a world where every healthcare practitioner, researcher, and diagnostician operates with the confidence that comes from using safe, effective, and rigorously tested tools. Our goal is not simply to provide products, but to enable results—to be the vital link that supports advances in diagnostics, treatment, research methodologies, and public health.

We pursue our mission through a holistic approach: by building a robust network of manufacturers and logistics partners, by staying ahead of technology trends and regulatory changes, and by investing in the expertise of our people. Biopex is committed to removing barriers and solving challenges—be it through custom procurement, expedited delivery, or technical consultation.

Our vision extends globally but is rooted in local impact. We actively seek to support health systems in underserved regions, facilitate international research collaborations, and provide educational resources to keep our clients informed and empowered. As the demands on medical and scientific professionals evolve, so too do our solutions. We continually expand our portfolio, focusing on products that are safe, sustainable, and aligned with contemporary priorities like digital laboratory management, point-of-care testing, and infection prevention.

Our Vision: To enable safer and more effective medical and laboratory environments globally.

Our Products and Services

Biopex offers an expansive range of medical and laboratory products designed to support healthcare, research, education, and industry. Our catalog includes laboratory equipment such as centrifuges, incubators, spectrophotometers, and PCR machines, as well as high-quality consumables ranging from test tubes and reagents to pipette tips and safety gloves. Our medical division covers diagnostic kits, surgical instruments, disposables, and specialized devices for sectors like pathology, hematology, clinical chemistry, immunology, and molecular biology.

We recognize that every laboratory and clinic is different, so we provide customizable procurement services. If you need a specific product not in our regular assortment, our sourcing team can locate and supply it quickly. Our logistics operations are optimized for reliability and speed, with robust inventory management and a well-developed distribution network that ensures prompt, safe delivery in India and worldwide.

Beyond products, Biopex emphasizes service: Our team provides installation, training, preventive maintenance, and technical support. We keep clients up-to-date about regulatory standards and offer guidance on instrument calibration, laboratory workflow optimization, and regulatory compliance documentation. For large-scale projects like new lab setups or modernization, Biopex offers consultation from planning and budgeting through commissioning.
