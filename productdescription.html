Laboratory Glassware Washers TGW-5000

The Laboratory Glassware Washer TGW-5000 is a high-capacity, automatic cleaning system designed for scientific environments needing reliable and efficient washing of laboratory glassware.

Laboratory Glassware Washers TGW-5000 is a double glass sliding door large glassware washer for cleaning both small and large items and increase workflow and output as a user can load 5 independent racks especially for laboratory and medical applications.

The TGW-5000 is decontaminating equipment that worked on moist heat to remove contamination and bacteria with a large wash cabinet and short program cycles. The key features include stainless steel interior, exterior, racks, and inserts withstand heavy use and provide protection against corrosion resistance materials and offer internal components peristaltic pumps and seals laboratory-grade for excellent durability of instruments.

Features

  • Chamber volume of around 202 liters supporting multiple layers (1-3) of glassware at once.
  • Adjustable circulation pump flow rate up to 600 L/min for powerful cleaning.
  • Hot air drying for rapid drying of glassware post-wash.
  • Standard feature for easy sorting and handling of glassware baskets.
  • Suitable for 144 seats of 25ml volumetric flasks, 84 seats of 100ml volumetric flasks, and 476 seats of sample or pipette vials.
  • Available in configurations such as 4 kW or 9 kW heating power.
  • Touchscreen control panel with programmable wash programs (up to 200 user-set and 12 factory presets).
  • Features conductivity monitoring which optimizes rinsing by measuring water purity and skipping unnecessary rinses.
  • USB/Ethernet enabled for exporting cycle data and reports.
  • Stainless steel chamber (usually 316L) and outer shell (304 stainless steel) for durability and corrosion resistance.
  • Secure, easy freestanding installation suited for laboratory environments.
  • Automated dosing of detergents and rinse aids for consistent cleaning quality.

Applications

Used for cleaning and sterilizing glassware such as beakers, flasks, test tubes, and petri dishes, ensuring a contamination-free environment and improving the accuracy and reliability of experiments.

Provides hygienic and thorough cleaning of medical laboratory glassware to maintain sterile conditions crucial for diagnostic testing.

Essential for cleaning pharmaceutical packaging containers and glassware to ensure drug safety, quality, and regulatory compliance.

Removes residual chemicals and contaminants from glassware to prevent cross-contamination and interference in subsequent experiments.

FAQ - Laboratory Glassware Washers TGW-5000

Que : What maintenance is required for the TGW-5000 to ensure optimal performance?

Ans : Routine maintenance includes daily inspection of filters, spray arms, and detergent levels. Weekly tasks focus on deep cleaning interior surfaces and running empty maintenance cycles. Monthly, descale the machine and inspect dosing systems and door seals. Quarterly, clean drying filters and validate performance. Regular maintenance prevents residue buildup, optimizes cleaning results, and extends equipment lifespan. Follow manufacturer guidelines closely for best results and contact authorized service providers for professional servicing when needed.

Que : Why does my glassware have spots or cloudy residue after washing?

Ans : Spots and cloudiness often result from mineral content in rinse water or excessive detergent use. Use deionized or purified water for rinsing and ensure detergent concentration is properly adjusted. Overloading the washer or using household detergents not formulated for lab washers can also cause poor cleaning outcomes. Regular cleaning and descaling of the washer prevent residue. Employing correct detergent and water quality maintains glassware clarity and hygiene.

Que : What should I do if the TGW-5000 washer fails to start?

Ans : Check that the washer is plugged in and receiving power. Make sure the door is fully closed and latched, as many washers have interlocks preventing operation if doors are open. Inspect fuses, circuit breakers, and look out for error codes on the display panel. If none of these resolve the issue, consult the user manual or contact technical support for professional diagnostics and repair.

Que : How can I extend the lifespan of my laboratory glassware washer?

Ans : Extend the TGW-5000’s lifespan with consistent preventive maintenance and proper use. Pre-rinse heavily soiled items to prevent corrosion from residues. Avoid overloading racks to allow efficient washing and drying. Use only laboratory-grade detergents designed for glassware washers. Follow daily, weekly, and monthly cleaning tasks diligently. Annual professional servicing ensures calibration and replacement of worn parts, preserving the washer’s performance and safety.

Que : Where can I get replacement parts and service support for the TGW-5000?

Ans : Authorized service centers and regional laboratory equipment distributors typically offer replacement parts and repair services for the TGW-5000. It’s advised to contact the original supplier or manufacturer’s certified partners to ensure genuine parts and expert servicing. Regular professional servicing is recommended to maintain optimal functionality and comply with safety standards. Keep purchase and service documents handy for warranty and support inquiries.
